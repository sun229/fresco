Our Story

Fresco started in 2004 with a small store in Newtown. From day one, we have stuck to our core tenents of integrity, high quality, sustainability and customer obsession.

We enjoyed and continue to enjoy superb growth by providing high quality casual wear at remarkably affordable prices for men and women.

Fresco is more than just a clothing brand, but a way of thinking. A steady consciousness of constant change, diversity, and the challenging of conventions.

Sustainability

Great products are created with sustainability at their core.
We are committed to having responsible purchasing practices that ensure we are a fair business partner.

It is our mission to bring ethical and eco-friendly designs to the masses at accessible price-points.
